Airport Service Design

Incheon International Airport(IIAC) is the largest airport in South Korea. IIAC has been selected as the Worldwide Best Airport by Airport Council International for eight consecutive years. I designed airport entertainments and complimentary services for transit passengers to improve customer satisfaction and profitability of the departure/transit lounge.

Role

  • Organized and implemented design projects for airport entertainments
  • Conducted questionnaire surveys and interviewed layover passengers and site staffs to evaluate service satisfaction
  • Managed third-party design agencies and handled outsourcing contracts
