The Main target users are young drivers with provisional licenses and their parents who want to monitor and protect their teen drivers.

Summary

I planned and conducted usability testing on their new swipe-through tutorial. The purpose of the study was to test their swipe-through tutorial for the new premium plan and see if the tutorial helps users understand Life360's new features and how it influences on users' purchase decisions.

I used a qualitative-quantitative mixed method and conducted in-person sessions with 24 participants for 3 different user groups(parent-mom, parent-dad, and Kids) in South Bay Area.

Methods

  • Qualitative: in-person usability testing
  • Quantitative: screening survey, questionnaires, and statistical analysis
