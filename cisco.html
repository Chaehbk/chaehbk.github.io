Summary

Cisco Learning Scinece team envisions next-generation learning for Cisco Networking Academy, a global IT-networking education program.

I'm the first user experience researcher for Learning Science team. As a UXR, I'm leading UX discussions by conducting the various types of design researches along the product development cycle.


Project 1. Problem-based Learning in VR

Cisco is trying to create a 3D immersive problem-based learning experience in real-life settings to complement traditional 2D education environments.

I'm a lead UXR for Cisco NetAcad VR and Cisco VR certification exam. I set up a research framework for the team and lead iterative design research on 3D immersive experience for online training and summative assessment.

Project 2. Online Distance Learning

I'm also leading user experience design and research works on rapid prototyping. After COVID-19, Cisco Networking Academy invests more in improving online teaching and learning experience. My team has a small group of engineers experimenting with many creative ideas to improve the quality of distance learning by applying problem-based learning, game-based learning, adaptive learning sequence design, etc.


Methods

  • Qualitative: Heuristic evaluation, Ethnographic study, Focus group, Moderated/Unmoderated usability testing
  • Quantitative: Survey at scale
