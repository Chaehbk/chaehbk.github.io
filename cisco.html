Summary

Cisco Learning Science team imagines next-generation job skill training experience for learners in the network engineering field. We are working on a VR-based problem-based learning experience that can complement traditional online learning environment.

I'm the first user experience researcher in Cisco Learning Engineering. As a UXR, I'm leading UX discussions by conducting the various types of design researches along the product development cycle.


Project 1. Problem-based Learning in 3D VR Space

We are building a 3D immersive problem-based learning environment in VR based on industry settings. My team believes hands-on exercise is critical for picking up hands-on skills for entry-level network engineers especially for beginner levels and entry-level engineers who have less or no industry experience. In Cisco NetAcad VR, learners would encounter real-life networking problems in the virtual data center and they are tasked to identify and troubleshoot problems.

I've been a lead UXR for Cisco NetAcad VR and Cisco VR certification exam. I'm working on iterative design research on 3D immersive learning experiences along the product development cycle.
