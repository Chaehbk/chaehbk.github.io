Summary

Cisco Learning Scinece team is trying to create a realistic problem-based learning experience in 2D and 3D spaces. My team envisions next-generation learning for Cisco Networking Academy, a global IT-networking education program.

I'm the first user experience researcher for Learning Science team. As a UXR, I'm leading UX discussions by conducting the various types of design researches along the product development cycle.

Project 1. Problem-based Learning in VR

Cisco is trying to create a 3D immersive problem-based learning experience in real-life settings to complement traditional 2D education/training environments. In Cisco NetAcad VR, users encounter real-life networking problem that they need to solve and they troubleshoot the problems as networking engineers would do in reality.

I'm a lead UXR for Cisco NetAcad VR and Cisco VR certification exam. I set up a research framework for the team and lead iterative design research on 3D immersive experience for online training and summative assessment.

Project 2. Online Distance Learning

I'm also leading user experience design and research works on rapid prototyping. After COVID-19, Cisco Networking Academy invests more in improving online teaching and learning experience. My team has a small group of learning scientists and software engineers experimenting with creative ideas to improve the quality of distance learning. I've been leading the user experiene design on problem-based learning, game-based learning, adaptive learning sequence design, etc.


Methods

  • Qualitative: Heuristic evaluation, Ethnographic study, Focus group, Moderated/Unmoderated usability testing
  • Quantitative: Survey at scale
