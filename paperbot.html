PaperBot

Summary

PaperBot is a edutainment paper toy which can be assembled without scissors, glue or chemicals. Kids can create their own paperbots and add up various physical movements using electronic modules. This product was developed for the purpose of providing a learning material for early science education.

Role

  • Designed paper toys and paper assebly kits
  • Developed scale mockups and interactive prototypes
  • Worked cross-functionally with design, engineering, and project management
  • Launched workshop and product demonstraion at Maker Faire Seoul 2015

Details

PaperBot includes a paper assembly kit and electronic modules(main board, input sensors, output units. Students can make an smart paper toy by folding the paper and attaching input and output modules.

Paperbot's first prototypes were broadcasted on YTN, 24-hour Korean news channel.
I demonstrated assembling process and illustrated operating principle.
IDLab with PaperBot was selected as the Best Maker
in 2015 by Korea Foundation for the Advancement of Science and Creativity.
