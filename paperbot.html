PaperBot

PaperBot is a small paper toy which can make movements using electronic modules. For my Master’s project, I worked with a Korean government organization under the Ministry of Education to make STEM education program in 2015-2016.

PaperBot includes ‘Product Design Kit’ and electronic modules (main board, input sensors, output units. Students can make an smart paper toy by folding the paper and attaching input and output modules.

Based on the smart product design kit, we developed animal-shaped paper toys, Jumping frog, Talkative parrot, and Dancing bear.

The first prototypes of the PaperBot were broadcasted on YTN, 24-hour Korean news channel.

IDLab with PaperBot was selected as the Best Maker in 2015 by Korea Foundation for the Advancement of Science and Creativity.
