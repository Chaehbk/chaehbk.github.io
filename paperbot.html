PaperBot

Summary

PaperBot is an edutainment paper toy that can be assembled without scissors, glue or chemicals. Kids can create their own interactive toys and add up various physical movements using our electronic module kit. PaperBot has developed for providing an interactive learning experience for young children.

Role

  • Designed three types of PaperBots and paper assebly kits - Bear, Crocodile, Parrot
  • Developed scale mockups and interactive prototypes
  • Worked cross-functionally with design, engineering, and project management
  • Launched workshop and demonstrated PaperBots at Maker Faire Seoul 2015

Details

PaperBot provides a paper assembly kit and electronic modules including a main board, input sensors, and output actuators.

Children create their own storyline using "if" conditional statements, and then choose the electronic parts to design a PaperBot accordingly following their scenario.
