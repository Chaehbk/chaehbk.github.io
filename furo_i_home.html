Furo i-Home

Summary

Furo i-Home is a voice-controlled home robot that connects with mobile phones and home appliances. I participated in UX researches on voice user interface design and hardware design improvement at close-to-production phase. I conducted multiple A/B testings to see how machine's voice-gender affects user perception depending on users' gender and task types. I also designed and executed the experimental study on relationship between the screen orientation and user engagement over the video call to improve screen rotation setting.

Role

  • Identified research topic and designed study that address user behaviors and attitudes
  • Designed and executed lab-based experiments using qualitative methods and a subset of quantitative methods
  • Designed and launched post-experimental surveys and conducted statistical analysis
  • Worked cross-functionally with design, engineering, and project management

Methods

  • A/B testing
  • Analysis of variance(ANOVA)
