Smart Birdhouse

A field study of STEAM education program development

Summary

ID Lab in Ewha Womans University developed STEAM education program for elementary schools that encourage eco-friendly learning activities in urban areas. STEAM is an educational approach to learning that uses Science, Technology, Engineering, the Arts and Mathematics as access points for guiding student inquiry, dialogue, and critical thinking. ID Lab suggested a 4-subject integrated education program based on 3rd-4th grade curriculums of science I, science II, mathematics, and art. This education program had developed through 8-month research and 6-week pilot program was condcuted for usability testing at Imae Elementary School with 2 teachers and 30 students.
